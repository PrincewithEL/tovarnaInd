About Us

Background

The company was started in 2019. Spending two years intensively researching the avocado oil industry and technologies used in production. Undeterred by the cost of production equipment, that is the industry standard, we partnered with a local engineering company which fabricated our current processing unit. We have been able to produce high quality oil, thus remaining competitive. We employ a B2B model where we sell our product to local companies which use our oil in their production.


Who We Are

A Kenyan company specialized in providing the highest quality avocado oil to our customers while empowering farmers in our supply chain. We ensure that the avocados used in our production are carefully selected to meet our quality standards, and that they are ethically sourced.


Our Vision

To be a globally recognised company that produces top quality extra virgin and crude avocado oil, from ethically sourced avocados. When you think of avocado oil, we want you to think of Tovarna.


Our Mission

At Tovarna, we aim to set the standards of excellence in avocado oil production and transform the lives of the farmers who work tirelessly to ensure we get the finest avocados.
